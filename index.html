EARN Initiative of GAEE JMI

The Association of Economics Education (GAEE) is a student-led international NGO founded in 2017 to promote economic education, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship incubation for students in developing countries and marginalized communities.

Through GAEE JMI, we aim to carry forward the vision to promote economic education, financial literacy, and entrepreneurship incubation for students with enthusiasm and vigour.
